Design Principles

✦

Minimal & Clean

Use whitespace generously. Let content breathe. Remove unnecessary decoration and focus on clear hierarchy.

≡

Consistent

Reuse components and patterns throughout. Maintain uniform spacing, typography, and interaction patterns across all pages.

♿

Accessible

Ensure sufficient color contrast, clear focus states, semantic HTML, and keyboard-navigable interfaces for all users.
